# Welcome to the Clapcheeks Alpha

You're one of the first people to try Clapcheeks — an AI dating co-pilot that automates swiping, sends smart openers, manages conversations, and books dates on your calendar.

Everything runs locally on your Mac. Your data stays on your machine.

## What to Do

### 1. Install (~10 min)
Follow the [Install Guide](./INSTALL_GUIDE.md). One terminal command gets you set up.

### 2. Run your first session
```bash
clapcheeks swipe --platform tinder
clapcheeks converse --platform tinder
```

### 3. Try the dashboard
Go to [clapcheeks.tech/dashboard](https://clapcheeks.tech/dashboard) to see your stats, conversations, and matches in one place.

### 4. Give feedback
After your first session, fill out the [Feedback Form](./feedback-form.html). It takes 3 minutes and directly shapes what we build next.

### 5. Join the group
The [Alpha Telegram Group](https://t.me/+clapcheeks_alpha) is where you can:
- Ask questions and get help
- Report bugs
- Share funny AI messages
- Suggest features

## Ground Rules

- **Don't share the install link publicly** — this is a closed alpha
- **Report bugs** in the Telegram group or feedback form — screenshots help a lot
- **Don't crank up the swipe limits** beyond defaults — it protects your dating app accounts from bans
- **Have fun** — this is supposed to make dating easier, not harder
